An interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built in Power BI to transform employee data into actionable workforce insights. The dashboard helps HR teams monitor attrition, understand employee demographics, and identify trends that support data-driven decision-making.

Key Features

Employee Attrition Analysis

Track overall attrition rates and identify patterns across departments, roles, and demographics.

Department & Job Role Insights

Analyze workforce distribution and compare employee counts across departments and job roles.

Demographic Breakdown

Explore gender diversity, age groups, and education fields through interactive visualizations.

Interactive Reporting

Use dynamic filters, slicers, and KPI cards to drill into specific workforce segments and trends.

Tools & Technologies

Power BI Desktop

Power Query for data cleaning and transformation

DAX for calculations and measures

Data Modeling and Relationship Management

Business Value

This dashboard enables HR professionals and management teams to monitor key workforce metrics, identify areas with higher attrition, and gain a clearer understanding of employee composition. The insights can support retention strategies, workforce planning, and overall organizational decision-making.
